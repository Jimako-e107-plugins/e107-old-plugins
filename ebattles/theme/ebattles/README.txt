Change //[menustyle]
To change the size of the left and right menus (larger/smaller), open theme.php and under the //[menustyle] section, look for:

<div style='width: 100%; text-align: center;'>
            <img style='margin-top: auto; margin-bottom: auto; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;' src='".THEME."images/blank.gif' width='160' height='0' class='ffimgfix' />


To do: Change the  width='160' (160 by default) to whatever u want to fit your site best:)
