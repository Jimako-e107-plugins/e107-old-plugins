## top_downloads_menu
### Top Downloads Menu

Author:  SecretR

This menu will show your latest 5 (or whatever you like) downloads on your e107 system. You can choose the downloads'category, how many downloads and for what period (week, month) to be shown.

source: SecretR

compatible: e107.org
